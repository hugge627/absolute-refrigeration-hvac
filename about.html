Our story

Built in Lubbock, trusted across West Texas

Absolute Refrigeration, Air Conditioning & Heating has proudly served the South Plains and Permian Basin for more than 18 years. As a family-owned and operated business, we’ve grown one satisfied customer at a time.

Our philosophy is simple: put the needs of our customers first. That means honest recommendations, upfront pricing, quality workmanship, and showing up when we say we will — whether it’s a routine tune-up or a 2 a.m. emergency.

As an authorized Trane dealer, we install equipment we believe in: efficient, reliable, and built to handle West Texas weather. Because, after all, it’s hard to stop a Trane.
